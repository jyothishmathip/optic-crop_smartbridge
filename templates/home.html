The problem

Farmers pick crops on habit — the land often disagrees.

01

Mismatched inputs

Nutrient and water needs vary widely by crop; the wrong pairing wastes fertiliser and yield potential.

02

Shifting conditions

Temperature, rainfall and humidity change season to season, but crop choice rarely adapts alongside them.

03

Limited feedback

Without a data-driven check, poor crop selection is only discovered after a failed or reduced harvest.

How OptiCrop helps

A trained model reads your field's numbers.

Enter seven soil and climate values and a Logistic Regression model — trained on agricultural production data across 20+ crops — returns the crop best suited to those exact conditions.

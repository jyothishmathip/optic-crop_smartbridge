About the project

Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine

OptiCrop develops an advanced software system that uses data-driven insights to optimize agricultural production for different crops. By integrating key environmental factors — Nitrogen (N), Phosphorous (P), Potassium (K) levels, soil temperature, humidity, pH, rainfall and crop types — OptiCrop provides intelligent recommendations to farmers for maximizing yields and resource efficiency.

The insights generated by OptiCrop can also be used by agricultural researchers, policymakers and stakeholders to advance the understanding of crop-environment interactions and inform evidence-based agricultural strategies — improving resource efficiency and contributing to sustainable farming practices.

Use cases

Three ways OptiCrop gets used

Smart crop recommendation for farmers

A farmer enters soil and environmental details — N, P, K, temperature, humidity, pH and rainfall. The system analyzes the data and recommends the most suitable crop for maximum yield and better farming efficiency.

Crop suitability and environmental assessment

A user checks whether current soil and climate conditions suit a particular crop. The application evaluates the inputs and reports on crop compatibility, environmental suitability and productivity potential.

Agricultural research and policy planning

A researcher or policymaker analyzes crop-environment relationships and identifies patterns in agricultural production, supporting data-driven, sustainable farming strategies and resource optimization.
